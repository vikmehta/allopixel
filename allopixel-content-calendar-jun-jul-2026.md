## Zero-Followers Strategy (Read This First)

**The honest truth about starting from scratch without Reels:**

Reels drive reach. But **carousels drive saves** — and saves are the strongest algorithmic signal that your content deserves to reach strangers. For a new account, one save-worthy educational carousel can reach 5,000+ non-followers. This calendar is built around that.

**Your two core content types:**
1. **Carousels** — Tip lists, before/afters, process breakdowns. These get saved and forwarded by business owners. Highest ROI for a zero-follower account.
2. **Static posts** — Bold statements, testimonial cards, palette posts. Fast to create, great for momentum and personality.

> **Optional: "No-face" process videos** — Screen recordings of design work (not face-cam) can be posted as Reels. No talking, no appearance — just a time-lapse of Illustrator/Figma in action over music. Marked as OPTIONAL in the calendar for the weeks you feel like experimenting.

**For Facebook with zero followers:**
FB feed posts reach almost nobody without an existing audience. The real game is:
- **Join 3–5 groups:** "Montréal Small Business Owners," "Canadian Entrepreneurs Network," "Quebec Business Owners," "Creative Business Owners Canada"
- **Add value in groups** (answer questions, share insights, then occasionally share a post)
- **Boost your best posts:** $20–50 CAD behind a strong portfolio carousel or educational post can reach 5,000+ targeted local business owners
- **Tag clients in portfolio reveals** — their network becomes your audience

**Posting Times (EST / Montréal):**
- Instagram: Tue/Wed/Thu — 10–11am OR 7–9pm
- Facebook: Mon/Wed/Fri — 9–11am

**Stories cadence (light, human, no pressure):**
- Mon: Reshare portfolio post → "New work ↑ What do you think?"
- Wed: WIP peek / poll ("Which direction: A or B?")
- Fri: Reshare post + "Questions? DM us 📩"
